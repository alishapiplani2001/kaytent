about us

We will provide you the best work which you dreamt for!

Established in 1967, KAY TENT Industries started it’s journey with manufacturing and supplying of Tents, Tactical Equipment such as High Altitude Rucksacks, Tents, Web equipment, Sleeping Bags to the Indian Army, UN & Para Military Forces. Kay Tent has developed itself as an OEM Manufacturer of backpacks to some of the leading luggage & Laptop brands. In all of our business segments, we provide unique services to meet your demands. Our superpower is our custom sewn products. Kaytent is one of the few Indian companies offering custom sewing services for almost any application. Our other services include- OEM contract sewing, manufacturing and product design. We at Kaytent can handle all the tasks with precision, always keeping quality first regardless of quantity.
